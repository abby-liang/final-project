Welcome to Abby's food blog!

Hello! My name is Abby and I am a big foodie.

I believe that good food can make a day better. Food can connect people, cultures, and communities together. Read more about diversity in food in the section below.


In this blog, I will show you some of my favorite foods that I have had eating in and eating out.

  • The Eating In page contains featured recipes (with images of my own cooking) to cook as a college student. Several recipes are my own and others are from online!
  • The Eating Out page showcases my food photography and cool food I have eaten and can't cook myself so be sure to check them out! You can sort and select favorites there, too.
